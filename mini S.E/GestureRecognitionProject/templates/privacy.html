Privacy Policy for Gestera

At Gestera, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Gestera, we may collect the following types of data...

Personal Information: This includes information such as your name, email address, and any other information you voluntarily provide when registering for the service.

Gesture Data: As part of the gesture recognition process, we may collect data related to your hand movements and gestures. This data is used to predict text or actions based on your gestures and is not shared or stored beyond the scope of your current session unless explicitly stated.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes...

Gesture Recognition: The main purpose of collecting gesture data is to provide you with an accurate and seamless gesture-to-text translation. This data is processed in real time and is not stored after the session ends, unless you explicitly request it.

Improving the Service: We use device and usage data to understand how users interact with Gestera, helping us improve features, performance, and overall user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously...

We take the security of your data seriously. All gesture data collected during your session is processed in real time and not stored after the session ends. Any personal information, such as your name or email address, is securely stored and encrypted in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data...

Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data if required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes, protect our rights, or ensure the safety of users.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time...

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new revision date.
